Student
Council

The Student Council is to provide students with opportunities for leadership development by organizing and carrying out school activities and service projects. In addition to organizing events that promote school spirit and community welfare, the student council serves as the student body’s voice. They aid in the dissemination of student ideas, interest, and concerns to the entire school community.
